## Pricing Models

### 1. Per-System Pricing

**Formula:** Base price + (Number of systems × Per-system rate)

**Example:**
```
Small Network (1-10 systems):
- Base: $3,000
- Per system: $500
- 5 systems: $3,000 + (5 × $500) = $5,500

Medium Network (11-50 systems):
- Base: $10,000
- Per system: $400
- 25 systems: $10,000 + (25 × $400) = $20,000

Large Network (51+ systems):
- Base: $25,000
- Per system: $300
- 100 systems: $25,000 + (100 × $300) = $55,000
```
